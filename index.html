About Us

At our yoga studio located in Seminyak, Bali our mission is to fill you up with positive energy. We provide daily yoga classes and spiritual wellness meditations with top instructors. Welcome to your Zen Zone.

Our Classes

Yin yoga

A Chinese form of yoga where by staying longer in various sitting and lying positions we find a deep relaxation and meditation. Suitable for those who strive for increased flexibility in musculature in combination with the breath and the meditative. Gentle for you with sensitive joints and a perfect class to take when you need to find your inner peace.

Ashtanga yoga

In the Ashtanga class, we move very dynamically and hold various static positions where we strengthen the entire body's musculature. Here we use discipline and focus as the key to meditation. Finally, you get a long shavasana to relax in.

Guided meditation for self-love 1hr.

Here, with the help of the noble art of hypnotherapy, we are guided into an inner journey to self-love. A trained yoga teacher and hypnotherapist, Tash Mind has conducted many life-changing sessions with those who have been open to deeply reprogramming the way they feel about themselves. This class is suitable for anyone who wants to increase self-love and thereby the quality of life.

Tapping 1hr.

This relatively new method has been created to awaken and release pent-up emotions. Here, by physically touching different parts of your own body, you get the opportunity to come into contact with feelings that have not yet been processed. Be prepared for a strong experience. For those who are ready to heal and let go in a safe environment.
